About

This Random Quote Generator is a simple web application that displays a new inspirational quote each time you click the button. It's designed to motivate and inspire you with a wide range of famous quotes. Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, it's a lightweight project perfect for beginners learning to code.

Privacy Policy

This app does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All quotes are stored locally within the code and no data is sent or received from a server. We respect your privacy and do not use cookies or tracking tools.
